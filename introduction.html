Marvel history

Marvel was started in 1939 by Martin Goodman under a number of corporations and imprints. However, Today it is known as Timely Comic and in 1951 it became commonly known as Atlas Comic. The Marvel era began in 1961. In 1961, the company launched titles for Fantastic Four and other superheroes such as Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, and Steve Ditko. The Marvel brand, which has been used for many years, has become established as the company's primary brand.
